### 1. brutalism — Brutalism / 粗野主义

- **Difficulty**: 2/5 | **Tags**: easy, light
- **DNA**: Roundness:0 | Contrast:5 | ColorRichness:2 | Complexity:2 | Warmth:1
- **Related**: mono, industrial, bauhaus

**Description (EN):** Raw, bold aesthetic with thick borders, high contrast and intentionally rough design. Great for portfolios and creative studios.

**Key Features:**
- Thick black borders (3px+)
- High contrast colors
- Hard offset shadows
- Monospace fonts + uppercase

**Color Palette:**
- `#000000` — Primary Black
- `#FFFFFF` — Base White
- `#FF3B00` — Accent Red
- `#FFDE59` — Highlight Yellow

**CSS Snippet:**
```css
border: 3px solid #000;
box-shadow: 6px 6px 0 #000;
font-family: monospace;
text-transform: uppercase;
```

**Hint:** Brutalism, thick black borders + hard shadows

**Prompt Templates:**
- **Basic Prompt:** Use Brutalist style: 3px+ thick black borders, hard-edge shadows (box-shadow: Npx Npx 0 #000), monospace fonts, uppercase titles, high contrast black-white + one bright accent color.
- **Advanced Prompt:** Design a Brutalist card: bright yellow background (#FFDE59), all borders 3px black, shadows use hard-edge offset (no blur), shadow shrinks on hover. Text monospace + uppercase. Convey an intentionally "unpolished" raw feel.
- **Keywords:** Brutalist, raw, thick borders, hard shadows, monospace, uppercase, high contrast, offset

**Do:** Use thick borders (3px+) · Pair with monospace fonts · Add offset shadows · Embrace raw feel

**Don't:** No gradients · No rounded corners · No soft shadows · No subtle colors
